## 1. Overview
Trianz utilizes the Internet as a critical resource for business processes. The company has developed systems and procedures to ensure that Internet usage is strictly for business purposes and conducted securely.

## 2. Scope
This policy establishes a framework to ensure all legitimate Internet use for the business needs of Trianz is met and that the utilization of organizational Internet resources is optimal.

## 3. Reference(s)
- IT Operations Process
- Anti-virus Policy

## 4. Rules

### Internet Communication
- All Internet communication must be logged, monitored, and audited periodically to prevent unauthorized access and ensure availability.

### Access
- All users are granted Internet access by default.
- The IT Department is authorized to provide Internet access to Trianz users upon joining, via a Service Request from HR.
- Access to specific sites is determined based on official work requirements.
- Users must enter their username and password to access any site on the Internet.

### Use of Non-Standard Internet Sources
- Only authenticated users may access the Internet.
- Dial-up access, PSTN, CDMA, GPRS, EDGE, GSM, or any other non-permissible means of Internet access are prohibited within Trianz office premises.
- Users authorized by the CIO/IT Manager to access the Internet on handheld devices must not use these devices to access the Internet on any device connected to the Trianz network.

### Use of Firewall, Proxy & URL-Filtering
- All Internet activities must pass through the firewall, proxy, and URL-filtering gateway.

### User Authentication & Authorization
- All Internet access is governed by Trianz Domain access and authorization.

### Gateway Level Virus Scan
- Anti-virus policies apply to all Internet activities.

### Internet Usage Code of Conduct
- Personal use of Trianz information systems to access the Internet is discouraged.
- Employees have no ownership or privacy rights to any information gathered via online resources.
- Trianz reserves the right to monitor all online communications, including email and Internet access. All information stored on company computers belongs to Trianz, which may inspect such computers and information as necessary.
- Accessing, downloading, or transmitting pornographic or obscene materials, or materials related to non-discrimination and harassment, including sexual harassment, is strictly prohibited.
- Trianz is not responsible for material viewed or downloaded by users from the Internet. Users access information at their own risk.
- Trianz reserves the right to record the locations of all Internet sites accessed by users and may share this information with any requesting party.
- The company reserves the right to block access to any Internet resources deemed to have no legitimate company purpose or that could negatively impact company computing resources (e.g., web mail, chat).
- Users must not engage in activities that noticeably affect system availability for others, such as online gaming or using web channels that frequently update.
- Users are prohibited from downloading screensavers, wallpapers, songs, music, videos, etc. This is considered an offense.
- Users may not upload any information to the Internet, except for official purposes and only after obtaining approval from the Head of Department or Director.
- Trianz reserves the right to take disciplinary action against users who violate these rules.
