## 1. Glossary
- **Hygro-Thermometer**: A device used to measure the relative humidity and temperature.
- **ASHRAE**: American Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air-Conditioning Engineers.

## 2. Temperature and Relative Humidity Measurement
- Temperature and relative humidity will be measured using a Hygro-Thermometer placed inside the data center.
- Security Personnel will note the measured details in the format specified in Appendix A, with a frequency of every 2 hours, 24/7, including weekdays, weekends, and company holidays.
- The acceptable ranges for temperature and relative humidity in the Trianz data center are:
  - **Temperature**: 15 °C to 25 °C
  - **Relative Humidity**: 20% to 80% RH
- If the temperature or relative humidity is found to be outside the allowable range, security personnel will inform the IT Administrator via phone.

## 3. Appendix A: Format of Temperature and Relative Humidity Measurement
- The format for recording temperature and relative humidity includes the following fields:
  - Date
  - Time
  - Temperature (°C)
  - Relative Humidity (%RH)
  - Name
  - Signature
