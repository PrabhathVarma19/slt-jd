1. Introduction
This document gives detailed guidelines on how the temperature and relative
humidity are measured in data center.

2. Temperature and Relative Humidity Measurement
The temperature and relative humidity will be measured by Hygro-Thermometer
placed inside the data center.
•

The measured details will be noted down by the Security Personnel in the
format mentioned in Appendix A, with a frequency of 2 Hrs. on all
weekdays and weekends including company holidays (24/7)

•

The acceptable range for temperature and relative humidity of Trianz
data center are as given below:
Measurement
Temperature (°C)
Relative Humidity(%RH)

•

Trianz Acceptable Range
15 °C to 25°C
20% to 80% RH

If the Temperature/Relative humidity if found to be out of allowable
range, the security personnel will inform the same to IT Administrator
through phone.
