## 1. Introduction
The ISAC is a representation of the people from relevant functions and business units and will meet quarterly to review the status and effectiveness of ISMS implementation.

## 2. Objectives
- Ensure that the organization’s Information Security Management System (ISMS) is established, implemented, maintained, and improved.
- Demonstrate the organization’s commitment to securing organizational and client information assets against vulnerabilities and threats.
- Ensure that all personnel apply information security in accordance with the ISMS framework.
- Management responsibilities include:
  - Briefing all personnel on their information security roles and responsibilities prior to granting access to the organization's information and associated assets.
  - Providing guidelines that state the information security expectations of their role within the organization.
  - Mandating fulfillment of the information security policy and topic-specific policies of the organization.
  - Achieving a level of awareness of information security relevant to their roles and responsibilities.
  - Ensuring compliance with the terms and conditions of employment, contracts, or agreements, including the organization's information security policy and appropriate methods of working.
  - Continuing to have the appropriate information security skills and qualifications through ongoing professional education.
  - Providing a confidential channel for reporting violations of information security policy, topic-specific policies, or procedures for information security ("whistleblowing"), allowing for anonymous reporting or ensuring that the identity of the reporter is known only to those who need to deal with such reports.
  - Providing adequate resources and project planning time for implementing the organization's security-related processes and controls.

## 4. Information Security Assurance Council Roles, Responsibilities and Authorities
### 4.1 ISAC Group
The members of this group will convene bi-annually to discuss the following:
- Status of the implementation of the information security policies and procedures.
- Security incidents reported and their resolution.
- Observed non-compliances to the Information Security Policy.
- Compliance with the policies and procedures of Trianz.
- Current and emerging threats relevant to the organization.
- Review of the Risk Assessment and updates to controls as needed.
- Inclusion of risk owners in ISAC group discussions to ensure aligned understanding and acceptance.
- Additional security requirements that require consideration.

### Changes in the Information Security Policy

- Personnel must be made aware of the Information Security Policy.

### 4.2 Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) / Chief Data Privacy Officer (CDPO)

- The CISO/CDPO has overall responsibility for Information/Cyber Security, Privacy, and Sustainability for Trianz.
- The CISO/CDPO sets a clear vision, mission, and direction for the Information Security, Data Privacy, and Sustainability Assurance Team.

### 4.3 Information Systems & Business Resilience Team

- Responsible for the organization's Security Posture.
- Implements improvements and initiatives related to Security Posture from IS operations.
- Aligns, delivers, and complies with Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management Systems.
- The Business Resiliency team focuses on processes, tools, and best practices for public sector business continuity and recovery, including technology assets, people, locations, and communications.

### 4.4 HR & Recruitment Team

- Conducts and manages the schedule for Information Security and Data Privacy Awareness Training for new hires during induction training/orientation.
- Ensures employee and candidate data is protected regarding Data at Rest and Data in Transit, Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA), and Background Verification (BGV).
- Takes disciplinary action against any Information Security and Privacy violations.
- Aligns, delivers, and complies with Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management System.

### 4.5 Professional Development (End User Training)

- Prepares an overall internal training plan in line with the Information Security Management System (ISMS) and submits it to the Head of Human Resources for approval.
- Plans, organizes, and monitors training programs.
- Prepares an annual budget for training.
- Locates and evaluates suitable faculty (internal or external) for training.
- Publishes Training Calendars.
- Sends periodic reports on training activities planned/executed to ITSM and the Head of Human Resources.
- Monitors the effectiveness of training activities against Trianz ISMS expectations and sends periodic reports to the Head of Human Resources.

### 4.6 Admin Team

- Ensures that physical security controls are implemented and comply with the Information Security, Data Protection policy, and Sustainability requirements.
- Aligns, delivers, and complies with Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management System.

### 4.7 Information Security and Data Privacy Assurance Group

- Ensures that Delivery and Business functions align, deliver, and comply with Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management System, including Trianz Information Security Policy and procedures, Data Privacy policies and procedures, and Sustainability Management System Policies and Procedures.
- Responsible for New/Existing Certifications, Internal and External Audits, and the organization's Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) and Business Continuity Planning (BCP).

### 4.8 Travel Team

- Ensures that data shared with vendors complies with necessary technical and organizational measures, and that vendors adhere to adequate security and privacy standards, including data minimization and purpose limitation.
- Aligns, delivers, and complies with Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management System.

### 4.9 Delivery and DA Team

- Ensures compliance with client Information Security and Privacy requirements.
- Aligns, delivers, and complies with Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management System.

### 4.10 Legal Team

- Responsible for the review and design of all Service Agreements, Non-Disclosure Agreements, and other applicable agreements.
- Aligns, delivers, and complies with Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management System.

### 4.11 Finance and Purchase Team

- Responsible for onboarding only Information Security approved business-critical vendors and Supplier Security Agreements.
- Aligns, delivers, and complies with Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management Systems.

### 4.12 Marketing Team

- Responsible for all technical and organizational measures regarding data subjects' security, including data at rest and data in transit (Prospective Clients) and Data Subject Access Requests (DSAR).
- Aligns, delivers, and complies with Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management Systems.

### 4.13 Platforms Team and Platforms Assurance Team

- Ensures that platforms are created, delivered, and comply with the organization's Information Security and Privacy Policies and Procedures.
- Aligns, delivers, and complies with Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management Systems.
