## 1. Purpose
- The purpose of this policy is to protect the intellectual property rights of Trianz customers, suppliers, cloud service providers, and any other material under the purview of IP rights.
- Intellectual property rights include:
  - Trianz technology, methodologies, and intellectual property worldwide, including but not limited to:
    - Products
    - Software tools
    - Hardware designs
    - Algorithms
    - Software (in source and object forms)
    - User interface designs
    - Architecture
    - Class libraries
    - Objects and documentation (both printed and electronic)
  - Any derivatives, improvements, enhancements, or extensions of the above conceived, reduced to practice, or developed during the term or in performance of this agreement that are not uniquely applicable to the client’s business processes and have general applicability in the art.
- This policy applies to all staff, including employees, contractors, suppliers, vendors, and interns working for or under the control of Trianz.

## 2. Objective
The objective of the policy is to establish guidelines and procedures for the management and protection of Trianz’s intellectual property assets in the context of digital innovation and transformation. This policy aims to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations, protection of customer data, and proper management of third-party intellectual property.

## 3. Scope
The scope of this policy is limited to information assets on which intellectual property rights of Trianz, customers, suppliers, and any other proprietary material are applicable.

## 4. Policy Statements
- Trianz ensures compliance with legislative, regulatory, and contractual requirements regarding the use of intellectual property rights.
- Trianz shall ensure its intellectual property rights are protected and clearly specified in all agreements with clients and vendors.
- Practices to protect intellectual property rights include:
  - Trianz shall not use illegal or improper means to acquire a competitor’s trade secrets or other confidential or competitive information.
  - Trianz shall respect and protect others' copyrights by not reproducing, distributing, or altering copyrighted materials without permission from the copyright owner.
  - Trianz shall acquire software only through approved vendors and reputable sources to ensure copyright compliance.
  - Trianz shall not duplicate, convert to another format, or extract from commercial recordings and images except as permitted by copyright law.
  - Trianz shall ensure that only authorized software and licensed products are installed. Cloud service customers must identify cloud-specific licensing requirements before permitting any licensed software installation.
  - Trianz shall maintain appropriate asset registers identifying all applicable assets to protect intellectual property rights.
  - Trianz maintains proof and evidence of ownership of licenses, master disks, manuals, etc.
  - Trianz implements controls to ensure that the maximum number of permitted users for intellectual properties is not exceeded.
  - Trianz provides awareness of policies to protect intellectual property rights and notifies personnel of the intent to take disciplinary action against breaches.
  - Trianz shall control the disposal or transfer of software to others in accordance with legal and contractual requirements.
  - Trianz shall comply with terms and conditions for software and information obtained from public networks.
  - All information, discoveries, developments, ideas, know-how, inventions, trade secrets, and/or original works of authorship developed or generated wholly or partially by employees during employment with Trianz, including all intermediate and partial versions, shall be the sole property of Trianz.

### 5. Breaches of Policy
- Trianz will take all necessary measures to remedy any breach of this policy, including the use of disciplinary or contractual processes where appropriate.
