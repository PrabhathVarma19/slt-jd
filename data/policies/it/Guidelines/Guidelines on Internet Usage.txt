  1. Overview
      The company utilizes Internet as a critical resource to carry on the business processes.
      Therefore, Trianz has developed systems & procedures to ensure that Internet is used
      only for business purposes in a secure manner.


  2. Scope
      To stipulate a framework for ensuring all legitimate use of internet for business needs of
      TRIANZ are met and the utilization of organizational internet resources is optimal.


  3. Reference(s)
      IT Operations Process
      Anti-virus Policy


  4. Rules
      All Internet communication is to be logged, monitored and audited periodically, for
      unauthorized access and ensuring availability.
             Access:
             All users shall be provided access to the internet by default. IT Department is
             authorized to give Internet access to Trianz users at the time of joining by a
             Service Request from HR.
             The sites that he/she is allowed to access on the Internet shall be based on the
             official work requirements.
             Users shall need to enter their username / password to access any site on the
             internet.
             Use of Non-Standard Internet sources:
             Only the authenticated users are allowed to access Internet. Users should not
             access internet via dial-up access, PSTN, CDMA, GPRS, EDGE, and GSM etc.
             or any other means as it is not permissible within office premises of Trianz.
             Users who have been authorized by CIO / IT Manager to access Internet on their
             hand-held computing devices should ensure that they do not use these
             computing devices to access Internet on any device connected to Trianz
             network.
             Use of firewall, proxy & URL-Filtering:
             All Internet activities shall pass through firewall, proxy and URL-Filtering
             gateway.
             User Authentication & Authorization:
             All Internet access is governed by Trianz Domain access and authorization.

             •    Internet Usage Code of Conduct:
             •    Use of Trianz information systems to access Internet for personal purpose is
                  discouraged.
             •    Employees have no ownership or privacy rights, to any information gathered
                  via online resources.
             •    Trianz has the right to monitor all online communication (Including email and
                  online access to internet). It is the policy of the company that all information
                  stored on company computers belongs to the company, and the company
                  may inspect all such computers and information at any time as necessary for
                  the conduct of its business.
             •    Trianz specifically prohibits the use of the Internet or other electronic
                  communications services or equipment to access, download or transmit
                  materials that can be interpreted as pornographic or obscene, concerning
                  non-discrimination and harassment, including sexual harassment.
             •    Trianz shall not be responsible for material viewed or downloaded by users
                  from Internet. The Internet is worldwide network of computers that contains
                  millions of pages of information. Users are cautioned that many of these
                  pages include offensive, sexually explicit, and inappropriate material. Users
                  access the information over the Internet at their own risk
             •    Trianz reserves the right to record the location of all Internet sites accessed
                  by users. Company reserves the rights in its sole discretion to share this
                  information or make public a complete listing of all sites visited by the users
                  to any requesting party.
             •    Trianz reserves the right to block users from any internet resources including,
                  but not limited to those which company determines in its sole discretion to
                  have no legitimate company purpose or which could have detrimental impact
                  on company computing resources i.e. web mail, chat etc.
             •    The users’ use of the system should not have a noticeable effect on the
                  availability of the system for other users. Therefore, a user should not
                  participate in online games, or have active any web channels that broadcast
                  frequent updates to his/her PC, such as the BBC News tickers, Cricket
                  updates etc.
             •    As per download policy, the users are not allowed to download any
                  screensavers, wallpapers, songs, music, videos etc. This shall be considered
                  an offence.
             •    Users are not allowed to upload any kind of information over the Internet,
                  except for information for official purposes only, after approval from Head of
                  Department / Director.
             •    Trianz reserves the right to take disciplinary action up to and including
                  termination against users who violate the Trianz’s Rules on Internet Usage.
