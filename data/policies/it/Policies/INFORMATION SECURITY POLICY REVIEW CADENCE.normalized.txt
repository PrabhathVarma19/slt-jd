## 1. Introduction
This document will detail the control objective-wise review cadence of Information Security Policies and Procedures.

## 2. Policy Review Cadence Schedule

1. **Control 5.1**  
   **Objective:** To provide management direction and support for information security in accordance with business requirements and relevant laws and regulations.  
   **Policy:** Information Security Policy Apex Manual  
   **Review Cadence:** Annual  
   **Triggers for Policy Review:** 
   - Internal Audit
   - External Audit
   - Client Audit
   - Client Visit

2. **Control 6.1**  
   **Objective:** To establish a management framework to initiate and control the implementation and operation of information security within the organization.  
   **Policy:** ISAC Roles and Responsibilities, Access Control Policy  
   **Review Cadence:** Annual  
   **Triggers for Policy Review:** 
   - Feedback
   - Management feedback

3. **Control 6.2**  
   **Objective:** To ensure the security of teleworking and the use of mobile devices.  
   **Policy:** Policy on the use of Mobile Computing and Communication Facilities, BYOD Policy  
   **Review Cadence:** Bi-Annual  
   **Triggers for Policy Review:** 
   - Changes due to Technological and Environmental Factors

4. **Control 7.1**  
   **Objective:** To ensure that employees and contractors understand their responsibilities and are suitable for the roles for which they are considered.  
   **Policy:** Background Verification Policy  
   **Review Cadence:** Annual  
   **Triggers for Policy Review:** 
   - Compliance to any new regulatory standards

5. **Control 7.2**  
   **Objective:** To ensure that employees and contractors are aware of and fulfill their information security responsibilities.  
   **Policy:** Professional Development Policy, Policy for Disciplinary Action  
   **Review Cadence:** Annual  
   **Triggers for Policy Review:** 
   - Security Incidents
   - BCP Triggers

6. **Control 7.3**  
   **Objective:** To protect the organization’s interests as part of the process of changing or terminating employment.  
   **Policy:** Asset Management Policy, Employee Exit Policy  
   **Review Cadence:** Annual  
   **Triggers for Policy Review:** 
   - Learnings from VA findings

### 8.1 Objective: Organizational Asset Protection
- The organization aims to identify its assets and define appropriate protection responsibilities.
- Relevant policies include:
  - Asset Management Policy (Annual Review)
  - Acceptable Usage Policy (Annual Review)

### 8.2 Objective: Information Classification
- The organization ensures that information receives an appropriate level of protection based on its importance.
- Relevant policy: Media Handling Policy (Bi-Annual Review)

### 8.3 Objective: Media Handling
- The organization aims to prevent unauthorized disclosure, modification, removal, or destruction of information stored on media.
- Relevant policies include:
  - Access Control Policy (Bi-Annual Review)
  - Password Policy (Bi-Annual Review)

### 9.1 Business Requirements for Access Control
- The objective is to limit access to information and information processing facilities.
- Relevant policies include:
  - Access Control Policy (Bi-Annual Review)
  - Password Policy (Bi-Annual Review)

### 9.2 User Access Management
- The objective is to ensure authorized user access and prevent unauthorized access to systems and services.
- Relevant policies include:
  - Access Control Policy (Bi-Annual Review)
  - Password Policy (Bi-Annual Review)

### 9.3 User Responsibilities
- The objective is to hold users accountable for safeguarding their authentication information.
- Relevant policies include:
  - Access Control Policy (Bi-Annual Review)
  - Password Policy (Bi-Annual Review)

### 9.4 System and Application Access Control
- The objective is to prevent unauthorized access to systems and applications.
- Relevant policies include:
  - Access Control Policy (Bi-Annual Review)
  - Password Policy (Bi-Annual Review)

### 10.1 Objective: Cryptographic Controls
- The organization ensures proper and effective use of cryptography to protect the confidentiality, authenticity, and integrity of information.
- Relevant policy: Policy on the Use of Cryptographic Controls (Annual Review)

### 11.1 Objective: Secure Areas
- The objective is to prevent unauthorized physical access, damage, and interference to the organization’s information processing facilities.
- Relevant policy: Physical Access Control and Environmental Security Policy (Annual Review)

### 11.2 Objective: Equipment Protection
- The objective is to prevent loss, damage, theft, or compromise of assets and interruption to the organization’s operations.
- Relevant policy: Acceptable Usage Policy (Annual Review)

### 12.1 Review Cadence
- The review cadence for policies is set to Annual.

### 12.2 Antivirus Protection
- Objective: To ensure that information and information processing facilities are protected against malware.

### 12.3 Backup
- Objective: To protect against loss of data.
  - Policies involved:
    - Backup and Restoration Policy
    - Data Retention and Secure Disposal Policy
    - Media Handling Policy
    - Password Protection Policy
    - Personal Data Protection and Privacy Policy

### 12.4 Logging and Monitoring
- Objective: To record events and generate evidence.
  - Policy: Information Security Logging and Monitoring Policy

### 12.5 Control of Operational Software
- Objective: To ensure the integrity of operational systems.
  - Policy: Patch Management Policy
  - Review Frequency: Bi-Annual

### 12.6 Technical Vulnerability Management
- Objective: To prevent exploitation of technical vulnerabilities.
  - Policy: Vulnerability Management Policy
  - Review Frequency: Bi-Annual

### 12.7 Information Systems Audit Considerations
- Objective: To minimize the impact of audit activities on operational systems.
  - Policy: Information System Audit Management Procedure
  - Review Triggers:
    - Internal Audit
    - External Audit
    - Client Audit
    - Client Visit Feedback

### 13.1 Network Security Management
- Objective: To ensure the protection of information in networks and its supporting information processing facilities.
  - Policy: Email Policy
  - Review Frequency: Bi-Annual

### 13.2 Information Transfer
- Objective: To maintain the security of information transferred within an organization and with any external entity.
  - Policy: Network Security Management Procedure
  - Review Frequency: Bi-Annual
  - Review Triggers:
    - Management Feedback
    - Changes

### 14.1 Security
- Objective: To ensure that secure software development life cycle (SDLC) practices are followed.
  - Review Frequency: Annual
  - Review Triggers: Due to Technological Changes

### 14.2 Security in Development and Support
- **Objective**: To ensure that information security is designed and implemented within the development lifecycle of information systems.

### 14.3 Test Data
- **Objective**: To ensure the protection of data used for testing in accordance with the Personal Data Protection and Privacy Policy and the Information Security Policy.

### 15.1 Information Security in Supplier Relationships
- **Objective**: To ensure protection of the organization’s assets that are accessible by suppliers.

### 15.2 Supplier Service Delivery
- **Objective**: To maintain an agreed level of information security and service delivery in line with supplier management agreements.

### 16.1 Management of Information Security Incidents
- **Objective**: To ensure a consistent and effective approach to the management of information security incidents, including communication on security events and improvements.

### 17.1 Information Security Continuity
- **Objective**: Information security continuity should be embedded in the organization’s business continuity management systems.

### 17.2 Redundancies
- **Objective**: To ensure availability of information processing facilities.

### 18.1 Compliance with Legal and Contractual Obligations
- **Objective**: To avoid breaches of legal, statutory, regulatory, or contractual obligations related to information security and any security requirements.

### 18.2 Objective: Information Security Reviews

- The objective is to ensure that information security is implemented and operated in accordance with organizational policies and procedures.
- Reviews will occur bi-annually and will focus on:
  - Changes due to technological and environmental factors.
  - Compliance with any new regulatory standards.
  - Security incidents.
  - Business Continuity Plan (BCP) triggers.

### 3. ISO Control Mapping(s)

- **Category of Control: Organizational Controls**
  - **ISO 27001:2022 Control 5.1**: Policies for information security must be defined, approved by management, published, communicated to relevant personnel and interested parties, and reviewed at planned intervals or when significant changes occur.
  - **ISO 27001:2022 Control 5.36**: Compliance with information security policies, rules, and standards must be regularly reviewed.
