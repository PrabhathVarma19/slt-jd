## 1. Introduction
The ISAC is a representation of the people from relevant functions and business units and will meet quarterly to review the status and effectiveness of the Information Security Management System (ISMS) implementation.

## 2. Objectives
- Ensure the establishment, implementation, maintenance, and improvement of the organization’s Information Security Management System.
- Demonstrate the organization’s commitment to securing both organizational and client information assets against vulnerabilities and threats.
- Ensure all personnel apply information security in accordance with the Information Security Management System framework.
- Ensure management responsibilities include:
  - Briefing all personnel on their information security roles and responsibilities before granting access to the organization's information and associated assets.
  - Providing guidelines that state the information security expectations of their role within the organization.
  - Mandating compliance with the information security policy and topic-specific policies of the organization.
  - Achieving a level of awareness of information security relevant to their roles and responsibilities.
  - Ensuring compliance with the terms and conditions of employment, contracts, or agreements, including the organization's information security policy and appropriate working methods.
  - Continuing to develop appropriate information security skills and qualifications through ongoing professional education.
  - Providing, where practicable, a confidential channel for reporting violations of information security policy or procedures ("whistleblowing"), allowing for anonymous reporting.

### 3. Organization Chart

#### 3.1 Information Security and Data Privacy Assurance
- The Information Security Assurance Council (ISAC) is led by the Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) and includes the Information Security Data Privacy Assurance (ISDPA) Team.

### 4. Information Security Assurance Council Roles, Responsibilities, and Authorities

#### 4.1 ISAC Group
The ISAC Group will convene bi-annually to discuss the following:
- The status of the implementation of information security policies and procedures.
- Security incidents reported and their resolutions.
- Observed non-compliances with the Information Security Policy.
- Compliance with Trianz policies and procedures.
- Current and emerging threats relevant to the organization.
- Review and update the Risk Assessment and controls as needed.
- Include risk owners in discussions to ensure aligned understanding and acceptance.
- Additional security requirements that necessitate changes in the Information Security Policy.
- Ensure personnel awareness of the Information Security Policy.

#### 4.2 Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) / Chief Data Privacy Officer (CDPO)
- The CISO/CDPO has overall responsibility for Information/Cyber Security, Privacy, and Sustainability for Trianz, setting a clear vision, mission, and direction for the Information Security, Data Privacy, and Sustainability Assurance Team.

#### 4.3 Information Systems & Business Resilience Team
- Responsible for the security posture of the organization.
- Implement improvements and initiatives related to security posture from IS operations.
- Align, deliver, and comply with Information Security, Privacy Management, and Sustainability Management Systems.
- The Business Resiliency Team focuses on processes, tools, and best practices for public sector business continuity and recovery, encompassing technology assets, personnel, locations, and communications.

#### 4.4 HR & Recruitment Team
- Conduct and manage the schedule for Information Security and Data Privacy Awareness Training for new hires during their induction training/orientation program.
- Ensure employee and candidate data is protected concerning Data at Rest and Data in Transit, Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA), and Background Verification (BGV).
- Enforce disciplinary action against any Information Security and Privacy violations.
- Align, deliver, and comply with Information Security, Privacy Management, and Sustainability Management Systems.

#### 4.5 Professional Development (End User Training)
- Prepare an overall training plan in line with the Information Security Management System (ISMS) and submit it to the Head of Human Resources for approval.
- Plan, organize, and monitor training programs.
- Prepare an annual budget for training activities.
- Locate and evaluate suitable faculty (internal or external) for training.
- Publish training calendars.
- Provide periodic reports on training activities to IT Service Management (ITSM) and the Head of Human Resources.

### 4.6 Admin Team
- Ensure that physical security controls are implemented and comply with the Information Security, Data Protection policy, and Sustainability requirements.
- Align, deliver, and comply with the Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management System.

### 4.7 Information Security and Data Privacy Assurance Group
- Ensure that Delivery and Business functions align, deliver, and comply with the Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management System, including Trianz Information Security Policy and procedures, Data Privacy policies and procedures, and Sustainability Management System Policies and Procedures.
- Responsible for New/Existing Certifications, Internal and External Audits, Organizations GRC, and BCP.

### 4.8 Travel Team
- Ensure that data shared with vendors complies with necessary technical and organizational measures, and that respective vendors meet adequate security and privacy standards by ensuring data minimization and purpose limitation.
- Align, deliver, and comply with the Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management System.

### 4.9 Delivery and DA Team
- Ensure compliance with client Information Security and Privacy requirements.
- Align, deliver, and comply with the Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management System.

### 4.10 Legal Team
- Responsible for the review and design of all Service Agreements, Non-Disclosure Agreements, and all other applicable agreements.
- Align, deliver, and comply with the Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management System.

### 4.11 Finance and Purchase Team
- Responsible for onboarding only Information Security approved business-critical vendors and Supplier Security Agreements.
- Align, deliver, and comply with the Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management Systems.

### 4.12 Marketing Team
- Responsible for all technical and organizational measures of data subjects' security, including data at rest and data in transit (Prospective Clients) and DSAR Requests.
- Align, deliver, and comply with the Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management Systems.

### 4.13 Platforms Team and Platforms Assurance Team
- Ensure that platforms are created, delivered, and comply with the organization's Information Security and Privacy Policies and Procedures.
- Align, deliver, and comply with the Information Security, Privacy Management System, and Sustainability Management Systems.
