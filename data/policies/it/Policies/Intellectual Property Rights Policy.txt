1. Purpose
   • The purpose of this policy is to protect intellectual property rights of Trianz
     customers, suppliers, cloud service providers and any other material under the
     purview of IP rights.
   • Intellectual property rights include:
   • Trianz technology, methodologies and intellectual property anywhere in the
     world (including, without limitation, product(s), software tools, hardware
     designs, algorithms, software (in source and object forms), user interface
     designs, architecture, class libraries, objects and documentation (both printed
     and electronic)) existing at the effective date or otherwise arising outside of
     work under this Agreement;
   • any derivatives, improvements, enhancements or extensions of the foregoing
     conceived, reduced to practice, or developed during the term or in
     performance of this agreement that are not uniquely applicable to the client’s
     business processes and or that have general applicability in the art.
   • This policy applies to all staff, including employees, contractors, suppliers,
     vendors and intern’s etc. working for, or under the control of Trianz.


2. Objective
   The objective of the policy is to establish guidelines and procedures for the
   management and protection of the Trianz’s intellectual property assets in the
   context of digital innovation and transformation. This policy aims to ensure
   compliance with applicable laws and regulations, protection of customer data
   and proper management of third- party intellectual property.



3. Scope
   The scope of this policy is limited to Information assets on which intellectual
   property rights of Trianz, customers, suppliers and any other proprietary material,
   wherever IP rights are applicable.


4. Policy Statements
  •   Trianz ensures compliance with legislative, regulatory and contractual
      requirements on the use of intellectual property rights.



TRIANZ INTERNAL                                                                      4
  •   Trianz shall ensure its intellectual property rights (Intellectual properties as
      defined above in Section1) are protected and clearly specified in all our
      agreements with clients and vendors.
  •   The below are some of the practices, which will protect intellectual property
      rights:
  •   Trianz shall not use illegal or improper means to acquire a competitor’s trade
      secrets or other confidential or competitive information.
  •   Trianz shall respect and work to protect others copyrights. Trianz shall do this
      by not reproducing, distributing, or altering copyrighted materials without the
      permission of the copyright owner.
  •   Trianz shall acquire software only through approved vendors and through
      reputable sources, to ensure that copyright is not violated.
  •   Trianz shall not duplicate, convert to another format or extract from
      commercial recordings and images other than permitted by copyright law.
  •   Trianz shall ensure that only authorized software and licensed products are
      installed. The cloud service customers shall have a process for identifying
      cloud specific licensing requirements before permitting any licensed software
      to be installed in a cloud service.
  •   Trianz shall maintain appropriate asset registers and identifying all applicable
      assets to ensure protecting intellectual property rights.
  •   Trianz maintains proof and evidence of ownership of licenses, master disks,
      manuals etc.
  •   Trianz implements controls to ensure that maximum number of users
      permitted are not exceeded for IPs.
  •   Trianz provides awareness of policies to protect intellectual property rights,
      and giving notice of the intent to take disciplinary action against personnel
      breaching them.
  •   Trianz shall control the disposal or transfer of software to others in accordance
      with legal and contractual requirements.
  •   Trianz shall comply with terms and conditions for software and information
      obtained from public networks
  •   All information, discoveries, developments, ideas, know how, inventions, trade
      secrets and/or original works of authorship developed or generated wholly or
      partially by employees during employment with Trianz including all
      intermediate and partial versions thereof, whether or not patentable,
      protected by copyright, or otherwise legally protectable (“Work Product”) shall
      be the sole property of Trianz upon its creation and, in the case of
      copyrightable works, upon its fixation in a tangible medium of expression. This

   Trianz will take all necessary measures to remedy any breach of this policy
   including the use of the disciplinary or contractual processes where appropriate.
